About us

We live in a world where development is emphasized both in a local level and international level. However, development projects sometimes do reflect its objective. Rapid economic development brings in technological advancement, but it also increases consumption which sometimes create unsustainable growth. Therefore, to allow development serve its development goal, and to tackle the challenges that current development are facing, DeCon wants to take an interdisplinatory approach. DeCon (DC Development Conference) is a Biennial conference that aims to create a platform for designer, architect, developer, and urbanist, different development fields to engage in furious conversation about development. This year, DC DeCon will focuses on city design. Locally, how city design build strong and sustainable and infrastructure and system that comply with sustainable growth? Internationally, how can city design serve as an resilient tool for resilience to tackle disaster and humanitarian crisis?
